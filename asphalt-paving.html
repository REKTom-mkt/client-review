Asphalt Paving FAQs

How much does asphalt paving cost in Orlando?
Asphalt paving typically costs $3-5 per square foot depending on the project size and base preparation needed. We provide detailed, itemized estimates at no charge so you know exactly what to expect.
How long does asphalt paving take?
Most residential driveways take 1-2 days to complete. Commercial parking lots vary based on size, but we provide timeline estimates upfront and work to minimize disruption to your business.
When can I drive on new asphalt?
You can typically drive on new asphalt after 24-48 hours. Full curing takes about 30 days, during which you should avoid sharp turns while stationary and be careful with heavy loads.
Should I repair or replace my asphalt?
It depends on the extent of damage. Isolated cracks and small potholes can usually be repaired. Widespread alligator cracking, significant settlement, or pavement over 20 years old may need replacement. We'll assess your situation and recommend the most cost-effective solution.
What's the difference between overlay and replacement?
An overlay adds a new layer of asphalt over existing pavement - it's faster and less expensive but requires a solid base. Full replacement removes the old asphalt and rebuilds from the base up, which is necessary when the foundation has failed.
Do you handle permits and inspections?
Yes, we handle all necessary permits and coordinate inspections as required by local jurisdictions. This is included in our project management services.
