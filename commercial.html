Commercial FAQs

How much does commercial parking lot maintenance cost?
Costs vary based on the size and condition of your lot. Sealcoating typically runs $0.08-$0.15 per square foot for commercial lots. Striping, crack sealing, and repairs are quoted separately based on the scope of work. We provide free, detailed estimates so you know exactly what to expect.
Can you work nights and weekends to avoid disrupting our business?
Yes, we regularly schedule commercial projects during off-hours to minimize impact on your customers and operations. Night work, weekend work, and phased approaches that keep portions of your lot open are all available options.
How often should a commercial parking lot be sealcoated?
Most commercial lots benefit from sealcoating every 2-3 years, though high-traffic areas may need more frequent applications. Regular sealcoating can extend your pavement life significantly and is far less expensive than premature replacement.
How long will my parking lot be closed during work?
For sealcoating, expect 24-48 hours of cure time before vehicle traffic. Striping typically dries in 30-60 minutes. Paving and major repairs may require longer closures, but we can often work in phases to keep portions of your lot accessible.
Do you handle ADA compliance requirements?
Yes, we ensure all striping work meets current ADA requirements including proper handicap space dimensions, access aisle widths, signage placement, and the required number of accessible spaces based on your lot size.
Can you provide a maintenance plan for our property?
Absolutely. We can assess your property and develop a multi-year maintenance plan that schedules sealcoating, crack sealing, striping refreshes, and repairs at optimal intervals. This proactive approach minimizes costs and prevents major problems.
