Frequently Asked Questions

Click a question to see the answer

How much does asphalt paving cost in Orlando?
Asphalt paving typically costs $3-5 per square foot depending on the project size and base preparation needed. Contact us for a free, no-obligation estimate tailored to your specific project requirements.
How long does asphalt paving take to complete?
Most residential driveways take 1-2 days to complete. Commercial parking lots vary based on size and complexity. We provide detailed timeline estimates upfront so you can plan accordingly.
When can I drive on new asphalt?
You can typically drive on new asphalt after 24-48 hours. Full curing takes about 30 days, so we recommend avoiding sharp turns, parking in the same spot, or placing heavy objects on the surface during that time.
How often should I sealcoat my driveway or parking lot?
We recommend sealcoating every 2-4 years, depending on traffic volume and weather exposure. Regular sealcoating can double the lifespan of your asphalt and save significantly on future repair costs.
Do you offer free estimates?

Are you licensed and insured?
Absolutely. Florida Sealcoating is fully licensed and insured, with over 20 years of experience serving Central Florida. We maintain all required certifications and carry comprehensive liability insurance for your protection.
