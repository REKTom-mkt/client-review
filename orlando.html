Orlando Asphalt FAQs

How much does sealcoating cost in Orlando?
Sealcoating in Orlando typically costs $0.15-$0.25 per square foot for residential driveways and $0.08-$0.15 per square foot for larger commercial lots. The exact price depends on surface condition, accessibility, and any repairs needed. We provide free on-site estimates for accurate pricing.
What's the best time of year for asphalt work in Florida?
In Central Florida, asphalt work can be done year-round thanks to our warm climate. However, we recommend scheduling sealcoating during drier periods (typically October through May) when there's less chance of rain delays. Temperatures should be above 50Â°F and rising for optimal curing.
Do you work on both residential and commercial properties?
Yes, we serve both residential homeowners and commercial property managers throughout Orlando. Our commercial services include parking lots, retail centers, HOA communities, apartment complexes, and industrial facilities. Residential services cover driveways of all sizes.
How long does a typical sealcoating project take?
Most residential driveways can be completed in a few hours. Commercial parking lots typically take 1-2 days depending on size. The surface needs 24-48 hours to cure before vehicle traffic, though we can often phase larger projects to minimize business disruption.
Are you licensed and insured in Florida?
Yes, Florida Sealcoating LLC is fully licensed and insured to perform asphalt services throughout the state of Florida. We carry comprehensive general liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage for your protection.
