Protective Sealcoating

Sealcoating is a protective barrier that is applied over the asphalt. It is an effective way to help you avoid making costly repairs later on. Sealcoating is the most efficient way to avoid water from penetrating into the asphalt and causing issues down the road.

We recommend sealcoating every 2-4 years to maintain optimal protection and appearance.

How much does asphalt paving cost in Orlando?
Asphalt paving typically costs $3-5 per square foot depending on the project size and base preparation needed. Factors that affect pricing include the condition of existing pavement, drainage requirements, and accessibility. Contact us for a free, no-obligation estimate tailored to your specific project requirements.
How long does asphalt paving take to complete?
Most residential driveways take 1-2 days to complete. Commercial parking lots vary based on size and complexity, ranging from a few days to several weeks for large projects. We provide detailed timeline estimates upfront so you can plan accordingly and minimize disruption to your business or home.
When can I drive on new asphalt?
You can typically drive on new asphalt after 24-48 hours. Full curing takes about 30 days, so we recommend avoiding sharp turns, parking in the same spot repeatedly, or placing heavy objects on the surface during that time. Florida's warm climate actually helps asphalt cure faster than in cooler regions.
How often should I sealcoat my driveway or parking lot?
We recommend sealcoating every 2-4 years, depending on traffic volume and weather exposure. In Central Florida, the intense sun and frequent rain can accelerate wear, making regular sealcoating essential. This preventive maintenance can double the lifespan of your asphalt and save significantly on future repair costs.
