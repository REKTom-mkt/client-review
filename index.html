Frequently Asked Questions

How much does asphalt paving cost in Orlando?
Asphalt paving typically costs $3-5 per square foot depending on the project size and base preparation needed. Contact us for a free, no-obligation estimate tailored to your specific project.
How often should I sealcoat my driveway or parking lot?
We recommend sealcoating every 2-4 years, depending on traffic volume and weather exposure. Regular sealcoating can double the lifespan of your asphalt.
Are you licensed and insured?
