Residential FAQs

How much does driveway sealcoating cost?
Residential driveway sealcoating typically costs $0.15-$0.25 per square foot, with most standard driveways ranging from $150-$400. The exact price depends on driveway size, condition, and any repairs needed. We provide free estimates with detailed pricing.
How long do I need to stay off my driveway after sealcoating?
We recommend staying off the surface for at least 24-48 hours to allow proper curing. Foot traffic is typically safe after 24 hours, but vehicles should wait 48 hours. Full curing takes about 30 days, so avoid turning your steering wheel while stationary during this period.
How often should I sealcoat my driveway?
For most residential driveways in Florida, we recommend sealcoating every 2-4 years. Factors like sun exposure, traffic levels, and previous maintenance affect this timeline. New asphalt should cure for 6-12 months before the first application.
Can you fix cracks in my driveway?
Yes, we include basic crack filling with our sealcoating service at no extra charge. For driveways with extensive cracking or larger damaged areas, we may recommend separate crack sealing or patching services before sealcoating for the best long-term results.
Do you work with HOAs and property managers?
Absolutely. We work with numerous HOAs and property management companies throughout Central Florida. We can provide bulk pricing for multiple properties, detailed documentation for board meetings, and flexible scheduling to minimize disruption to residents.
What if it rains on the day of my appointment?
Sealcoating requires dry conditions to properly adhere and cure. If rain is expected, we'll contact you to reschedule at your earliest convenience. There's no charge for weather-related rescheduling, and we monitor forecasts closely to avoid surprises.
How do I prepare my driveway for sealcoating?
Simply remove all vehicles and clear any items from the driveway. We handle all the cleaning and preparation work, including removing dirt, debris, and treating oil spots. If you have sprinklers, please turn them off for 24 hours before and after the service.
