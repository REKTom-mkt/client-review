Frequently Asked Questions

Get straightforward answers to common questions about the paving process, project timelines, and what to expect when upgrading your commercial parking lot.

How long will a new asphalt parking lot last?

With proper installation and regular maintenance, an asphalt parking lot can last 15-30 years. The key factors are proper base preparation, quality materials, and ongoing maintenance including sealcoating every 2-3 years and prompt crack repair.

Can you pave my parking lot while my business stays open?

Yes, our professional field operators work quickly and efficiently to minimize disruption to your business. We can work in phases to keep portions of your lot accessible, schedule work during off-hours or weekends, or develop a custom plan based on your specific needs.

What's the difference between asphalt overlay and full replacement?

An asphalt overlay adds a new layer over existing pavement and works well when the base is still structurally sound. Full replacement removes the old surface down to the base and is necessary when there's significant structural damage. We'll assess your lot and recommend the most cost-effective solution.

Do you handle ADA compliance and signage?

Yes, we ensure all parking lot projects meet ADA compliance requirements including proper handicap space dimensions, signage, access aisles, and slope requirements. We also handle sign installation for handicapped parking and stop signs as part of our full-service approach.
