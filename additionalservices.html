Concrete Curbs

Concrete curbs are essential for defining parking areas, protecting landscaping, and directing traffic flow throughout your property. Our professional curb installation services help maintain a well-organized and visually appealing parking lot.

Whether you need new curb installation, curb repairs, or complete replacement of damaged sections, our experienced team delivers durable results that withstand Florida's climate and heavy traffic conditions.

  • Traffic Control: Direct vehicles and define parking boundaries
  • Landscape Protection: Prevent vehicles from damaging grass and plants
  • Water Management: Channel runoff to proper drainage areas
  • ADA Compliance: Install compliant curb cuts and ramps

Speed Bump Installation

Speed bumps and speed humps are critical safety features for parking lots, helping to slow down traffic and protect pedestrians. We install both permanent asphalt speed bumps and removable rubber speed bumps to meet your specific needs.

Proper speed bump placement and installation is essential for effectiveness without causing vehicle damage. Our team assesses your lot's traffic patterns to recommend optimal locations and sizes.

  • Pedestrian Safety: Reduce vehicle speeds in high-traffic areas
  • Multiple Options: Asphalt, concrete, or rubber speed bumps available
  • Custom Sizing: Appropriate heights for different speed requirements
  • Reflective Marking: High-visibility striping for nighttime safety

Car Stops (Wheel Stops)

Car stops, also known as wheel stops or parking blocks, prevent vehicles from overhanging into walkways, landscaping, or other parking spaces. We install new car stops and repair or replace damaged ones throughout Orlando and Central Florida.

Available in concrete or rubber options, our car stops are securely anchored to withstand repeated impacts. We also offer bright yellow striping for maximum visibility.

  • Pedestrian Protection: Prevent vehicles from blocking sidewalks
  • Property Protection: Keep cars from hitting walls and landscaping
  • Durable Materials: Concrete or heavy-duty rubber options
  • Repair Service: Re-anchor or replace damaged wheel stops

Pressure Washing

Professional pressure washing removes oil stains, dirt, mold, mildew, and other buildup from your parking lot, sidewalks, and concrete surfaces. Regular cleaning improves appearance, prevents slip hazards, and prepares surfaces for sealcoating or striping.

Our commercial-grade equipment handles everything from small driveways to large commercial parking lots. Pressure washing is also an essential preparation step before sealcoating for optimal adhesion and results.

  • Surface Preparation: Essential before sealcoating or striping
  • Oil Stain Removal: Remove automotive fluid stains
  • Curb Appeal: Restore bright, clean appearance
  • Safety: Remove slippery mold and algae buildup

Asphalt Milling

Asphalt milling removes the top layer of deteriorated asphalt, creating a clean, level surface for new paving. This process is more cost-effective than complete removal and allows for proper drainage and smooth transitions at curbs and buildings.

Milling is ideal for parking lots with surface damage but a sound base. The removed material can often be recycled, making it an environmentally responsible choice for pavement rehabilitation.

  • Cost Effective: Less expensive than full depth removal
  • Level Surface: Creates smooth transitions at drains and curbs
  • Eco-Friendly: Recycled asphalt millings can be reused
  • Preparation: Ideal base for new asphalt overlay

Additional Services FAQs

Do you provide free estimates for all additional services?
Yes, we provide free, no-obligation estimates for all our services including curb installation, speed bumps, signs, car stops, pressure washing, and more. We'll assess your property and provide detailed pricing before any work begins.
How long does speed bump installation take?
Most speed bump installations can be completed in a single day. Rubber speed bumps can typically be installed and ready for use immediately, while asphalt or concrete speed bumps may require 24-48 hours to cure before traffic can resume.
What type of parking signs do you install?
We install all types of parking lot signage including handicap parking signs, fire lane signs, no parking signs, reserved parking signs, directional arrows, stop signs, speed limit signs, and custom business signage. All signs meet ADA and local code requirements.
How often should car stops be replaced?
Concrete car stops typically last 10-15 years with proper maintenance. However, they may need repair or replacement sooner if they become cracked, shifted, or loose. We recommend annual inspections to identify any wheel stops that need attention.
Is pressure washing necessary before sealcoating?
Pressure washing is highly recommended before sealcoating as it removes dirt, oil, and debris that can prevent proper adhesion. A clean surface ensures the sealcoat bonds effectively and lasts longer. We include surface preparation in our sealcoating services.
Can you add parking spaces by removing islands?
Yes, removing unused or oversized parking lot islands is a common way to gain additional parking spaces. However, local codes may require a minimum number of landscape islands. We'll help you understand requirements and maximize your parking capacity within regulations.
Do you repair sidewalk trip hazards?
Absolutely. We offer several solutions for sidewalk trip hazards including grinding down raised edges, mud-jacking sunken sections, and replacing damaged concrete panels. Addressing trip hazards promptly helps prevent injuries and potential liability issues.
What areas do you serve for additional services?
We provide all our additional parking lot services throughout Orlando and Central Florida, including Orange, Seminole, Osceola, Lake, and Volusia counties. Contact us to confirm service availability in your specific location.
